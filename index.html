About Us

Moderna Homes Pte Ltd is a leader in modular high-rise construction, specializing in Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction (PPVC). Based in Singapore, we are at the forefront of building smarter, more sustainable, and safer buildings for the region.

Why Choose PPVC Construction?
Accelerated Completion
We built faster than conventional methods, saving you time
Enhanced Workplace Safety
With most construction happening off-site, the risk at ground level is minimized.
Higher Productivity
Better deployment, supervision, and monitoring improve overall efficiency.
Superior Quality
Our prefabricated units have higher internal finishing quality and overall durability.
Less Weather Dependency
Progress is not halted by inclement weather, reducing project delays.
Lower Manpower Requirements
Our method reduces the need for on-site labor, lowering costs.
Eco-Friendly
With a smaller carbon footprint and reduced noise and pollution, our approach is more environmentally conscious.
