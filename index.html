MODULAR HIGHRISE CONSTRUCTION
Moderna Homes Pte Ltd

is a champion of and specialist in modular highrise construction in Singapore and the region. Out sterling track record in Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction (PPVC) has positioned us as one of the top-of-the-mind choice in the construction industry.

Key advantages of PPVC:
  • Accelerated building completion compared to conventional constructions.
  • Higher workplace safety as all works are done within the factory and ground level.
  • Higher productivity due to better workmen deployment, supervision and monitoring.
  • Higher construction quality and better internal finishing.
  • Progress less affected by inclement weather.
  • Lower manpower requirements.
  • Less noise and pollution generated on-site.
  • Smaller carbon footprint leading to a more environmentally friendly construction methodology.
