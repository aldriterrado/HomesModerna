Frequently Asked Questions Everything You Need to Know About Our Services and Solutions

What is Modular High-Rise Construction?

Modular high-rise construction involves building pre-fabricated, prefinished volumetric units off-site in a controlled factory environment. These units are then transported and assembled on-site to create high-rise buildings. This method offers greater efficiency, faster construction times, and improved quality control.

What are the benefits of Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction (PPVC)?

PPVC allows for faster project completion, reduced labor costs, improved safety standards, and a more sustainable approach to construction by reducing material waste. It also ensures higher precision and quality as the modules are manufactured in a controlled environment.

What types of buildings can you construct using modular construction?

We specialize in constructing high-rise residential, commercial, and mixed-use buildings. Our modular construction solutions are adaptable to various building types, ensuring flexibility in design and functionality.

What certifications and accreditations does Moderna Homes have?

Moderna Homes is accredited under the Building and Construction Authority’s (BCA) Manufacturer Accreditation Scheme (MAS) for our factories in Malaysia and Singapore. Our PPVC design system is also approved by Hong Kong’s Building Department's IPA scheme.

How long does it take to complete a modular high-rise project?

The construction timeline depends on the project's complexity and scale. However, modular construction typically allows for faster completion compared to traditional methods, with the potential to reduce construction time by 20% or more.

What is the cost difference between modular construction and traditional construction?

While the initial cost of modular construction may be similar to traditional construction, the overall project cost can be lower due to reduced labor, shorter project timelines, and improved efficiency. Additionally, savings are achieved through fewer material wastages.

How does modular construction impact sustainability?

Modular construction reduces waste, energy consumption, and carbon footprint. The prefabrication process is more efficient, and the ability to recycle materials contributes to a more sustainable building process.

Can modular buildings be customized?

Yes, modular buildings can be customized to meet specific design and functionality requirements. We offer a range of flexible design options that can be tailored to suit the unique needs of each project.

What are the safety standards followed during construction?

We adhere to strict safety protocols both in our factories and on-site. Our modular construction process is designed to minimize on-site risks, and our buildings meet or exceed local building codes and regulations for safety and quality.

Do you offer post-construction services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-construction services including maintenance, repairs, and ongoing support to ensure the longevity and performance of the buildings we create.
